Passion

This has shaped the way i view life and teh challeneges that come with it; i encourage it, with the view that with each challenge, I get better and better. This mentality has gotten me through many late nights doing projects and many failed quizes or exams. It has kept me going and striving.

My passion has led me to seek out others like me. Because we share this passion we are able to work together and do great things. This carries over into my academic life where i am able to leverage my passion in group settings and class discussions.

My passion has also birthed compassion and a spirit of giving. This has led me to engage in several in school volunteery acts to support pewople who are lesser fortunate.

Through these volunteering oppurtunities i try to spread my passion and knowledge to those willing.

My passion has helped me seek out learning oppurtunities outside of school that would help me be better in school.
